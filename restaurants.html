Denmark Tavern - Serampore

This was once in complete ruins and after its restoration is now a restaurant and one of the sought-after destinations in Serampore. In 1786 this place was a lodge along with a café. Serampore being the Danish post was out of the English jurisdictions thus this place became infamous during its time for people wanting a quick getaway place.

Chowman - Serampore

It is one of the most well-known Chinese restaurants in Kolkata. I won’t say they serve authentic Chinese dish, but they indeed serve authentic Indo-Chinese dishes. They have their versions of everything. Their dumplings and noodles are delicious. Their chicken Manchurian and garlic chicken is also trendy.
